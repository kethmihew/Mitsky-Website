Our Protagonist

Meet Mitsky. She’s trying to improve herself — to study more, work smarter, and maybe even shower on time — and we’re here to help her do just that. Because, truthfully, there’s a little bit of Mitsky in all of us. Whether it’s procrastination, low motivation, or everyday struggles, Mitsky is here to support users step step on their journey to better habits.

Our Story

We’re a team of first- and second-year Computer Science students brought together by a shared curiosity and a drive to create. With little experience but a lot of passion, we entered our very first hackathon — eager to challenge ourselves, learn new skills, and bring our ideas to life.

What started as a fun idea quickly grew into a project we’re truly proud of. Our teamwork, creativity, and determination paid off when Mitsky won 2nd place overall at the Beginner Hackathon. But this is just the beginning — Mitsky is more than an app; she’s a reminder that change is possible, and that small steps can lead to big growth.
